Fishing in Northwest Territories

Click a region to explore top fishing spots, species, and local tips.

The Northwest Territories are home to Great Bear Lake and Great Slave Lake, two of the greatest lake trout fisheries on Earth, plus world-class Arctic char, Arctic grayling, northern pike, and the mighty Mackenzie River.

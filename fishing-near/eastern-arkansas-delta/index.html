Fishing in Eastern Arkansas & Delta

Mississippi River, Big Lake, Crown Lake, St. Francis River, and the waters of the Arkansas Delta.

1. Mississippi River (Blue Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Largemouth Bass, White Bass)

Mississippi River, eastern Arkansas border

The Mississippi River offers excellent blue catfish and flathead catfish fishing along Arkansas's eastern border. The river's main channel and backwaters provide diverse fishing.

2. Big Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream)

Big Lake, Mississippi County

Big Lake offers excellent largemouth bass and crappie fishing in the Mississippi River floodplain near Blytheville.

3. Crown Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bream, Catfish)

Crown Lake, Mississippi County

Crown Lake offers good bass and bream fishing in the Delta region.

4. St. Francis River (Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

St. Francis River, eastern Arkansas

The St. Francis River offers good catfish and bass fishing along the Arkansas-Missouri border.

5. White River (Lower) (Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Lower White River, eastern Arkansas

The lower White River through the Delta offers excellent blue catfish and flathead catfish fishing.

6. Arkansas River (Lower) (Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, White Bass)

Lower Arkansas River, eastern Arkansas

The lower Arkansas River through the Delta offers excellent catfish and white bass fishing.

7. Lake Chicot (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream)

Lake Chicot, Chicot County

Lake Chicot is Arkansas's largest natural lake, an oxbow of the Mississippi River offering excellent crappie and bass fishing.

8. Horseshoe Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream)

Horseshoe Lake, Crittenden County

Horseshoe Lake is another Mississippi River oxbow offering good bass and crappie fishing near West Memphis.

9. Mallard Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Mallard Lake, Crittenden County

Mallard Lake offers good bass and crappie fishing in the Delta.

10. Beaver Lake (Delta) (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Beaver Lake, Crittenden County

Beaver Lake in the Delta offers good bass and crappie fishing.

11. Bear Creek Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Bear Creek Lake, Jackson County

Bear Creek Lake offers good bass and crappie fishing near Newport.

12. Lake Poinsett (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Lake Poinsett, Poinsett County

Lake Poinsett offers good bass and crappie fishing near Harrisburg.

13. Strawberry River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass)

Strawberry River, Lawrence County

The Strawberry River offers good smallmouth bass fishing in the eastern Arkansas hills.

14. Spring River (Lower) (Smallmouth Bass, Catfish)

Lower Spring River, Lawrence County

The lower Spring River offers good smallmouth bass and catfish fishing.

15. Black River (Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Black River, eastern Arkansas

The Black River offers excellent catfish and bass fishing in the Delta region.

16. Bear Creek Lake (Delta) (Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Bear Creek Lake, Jackson County

Bear Creek Lake offers good fishing.

17. Walnut Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Walnut Lake, Jackson County

Walnut Lake offers good fishing.

18. Eagle Lake (Delta) (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Eagle Lake, Crittenden County

Eagle Lake offers good fishing.

19. Old Town Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Old Town Lake, Craighead County

Old Town Lake offers good fishing.

20. Big Creek (Delta) (Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Big Creek, Cross County

Big Creek offers good fishing.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at agfc.com.
  • Fish the Mississippi for cats -- The Mississippi River offers world-class blue catfish and flathead catfish fishing.
  • Try oxbow lakes -- The Mississippi River oxbow lakes like Chicot and Horseshoe offer excellent bass and crappie fishing.
  • Spring sauger -- The lower Arkansas River offers good sauger and white bass fishing in spring.
  • Watch for current -- The Mississippi River has strong currents and frequent barge traffic. Fish with experienced guides.
